Our Story

Vigilance You Can Count On

HM Security was founded to raise the standard of security service across the Greater Toronto Area. We saw too many properties left with absent guards, slow response and impersonal service — and we set out to do it differently.

Today we protect condominiums, businesses, construction sites and events throughout the GTA with licensed, professionally trained officers and hands-on management that is always reachable. When you call HM Security, you're not just hiring a guard — you're gaining a partner invested in your safety.

We're small enough to care about every client and structured enough to deliver reliable, round-the-clock coverage. That balance is the heart of everything we do.
