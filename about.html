Our Story

Workmanship You Can Count On

HM Security Solutions was founded to raise the standard of security-system installation across the Greater Toronto Area. We saw too many jobs left with messy cabling, cut corners and poor after-service — and we set out to do it differently.

Today we design and install networking, alarm, camera, audio and access-control systems for homes and businesses throughout the GTA — with certified technicians and hands-on owners who actually answer the phone. When you call HM Security Solutions, you're not just hiring an installer — you're gaining a partner invested in your safety.

We're small enough to care about every client and skilled enough to deliver clean, reliable installations backed by real support. That balance is the heart of everything we do.
